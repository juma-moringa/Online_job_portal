From start

Our face has changed a bit over the years, but our mission hasn’t. We are dedicated to providing job seekers with the clearest path to their next amazing opportunity. That mission is written in our DNA. It’s who we are. It’s what drives us. Because we know it’s more than a career move, it’s helping people find their purpose.

Where we're heading

We got our start more than two decades ago as JobDig, an employment focused media company. When job search moved online, so did we. We saw huge potential in focusing on company websites as the best and cleanest source of jobs, so we formed LinkUp, a job site with millions of verified listings sourced directly from company career sites. As we developed our proprietary technology that scours the web for the freshest listings, every single day–savvy job seekers and employers noticed.

Our only aim

Now, we as linkup, we’re evolving our mission even further. This is only the beginning. In this next phase, we’re building a platform tailored for how people search, now and in the future. We’re innovating and updating; refining and streamlining, all to connect job seekers with the highest quality, most up-to-date job listings on the web. All to help people Get the best available job.
